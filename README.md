# Shopping Behaviour Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## üìå Clear Problem Statement
Retail businesses often struggle to understand customer shopping behaviour across demographics, regions, seasons, and product categories.  
The goal of this project is to analyze customer purchase data to identify spending patterns, high-value customers, and sales trends in order to support better marketing, pricing, and inventory decisions.

---

## üìÇ Data Source
- Customer shopping dataset (CSV / Excel)
- Data includes:
  - Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Age Group)
  - Product categories and items
  - Purchase amount
  - Region and season
  - Subscription status
  - Discount applied
  - Purchase frequency
  - Customer ratings

> The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Excel Power Query before analysis.

---

## üß† Logic / Analysis Approach
1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**
   - Removed inconsistencies in categories and frequency values
   - Created calculated columns (Age Group, standardized purchase frequency)
   - Ensured numeric consistency for purchase amounts

2. **Data Modeling**
   - Built Pivot Tables for:
     - Category-wise revenue
     - Region and season analysis
     - Customer demographics analysis
     - Purchase frequency trends
   - Created calculated KPIs (Total Revenue, Customer Count, Ratings Count)

3. **Dashboard Creation**
   - Designed interactive Excel dashboard
   - Used slicers for:
     - Season
     - Region
     - Age Group
     - Subscription status
     - Discount applied
   - Added KPI cards and charts for quick insights

---

## üîç Key Insights
- Clothing and Accessories generate the highest total revenue
- Adult customers contribute more to total spending than teenagers
- West region is the top spending region
- Fall season records the highest total purchase amount
- Customers purchasing quarterly and bi-weekly show higher average spending
- Subscribed customers demonstrate more consistent purchasing behavior
- High-rated products (4‚Äì5 stars) correlate with better sales performance

---

## üí° Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns during high-performing seasons (especially Fall)
- Target high-value regions (West) with personalized offers
- Promote subscription plans to increase repeat purchases
- Prioritize inventory for top-performing categories and products
- Design loyalty programs for high-frequency and high-spending customers
- Use customer ratings to guide product promotions and improvements
